Mission Statement

The primary goal of the new website is to increase revenue from the key income streams; Membership Sales, and Orchestra performance attendance, by 50% combined.

In addition to the primary goal, two core requirements have also been identified:

Core Requirements

  • Attract a younger clientele base.
  • Simplify becoming a Member Online.

Success Evaluation

Success evaluation will be measured as a direct increase in online membership sales, and Orchestra attendance numbers. Currently, Orchestra ticket sales are outsourced to Ticketshop with Barrier Reef Orchestra receiving a share of the ticket sales for each performance. Whereas, Membership sales is handled inhouse from the current website.

Both Membership sales, and Orchestra Attendance revenue streams allow quantifiable measurements to track revenue increase. It is expected the Goal of a combined 50% revenue increase be reached within twelve months for the website to be deemed successful.

Target Audience

Who

Historically, the Barrier Reef Orchestra has attracted music lovers from many of Townsville’s social groups:

  • Music Teachers, seeking to hone and display their skills.
  • Defense Families, often linked to the military band.
  • Doctors and Medical students, keen to show off their skills.
  • Lawyers and other Professionals, seeking to engage in cultural philanthropy.
  • Hight School Students, eager to play at the next level.

In addition to local talent, The Barrier Reef orchestra regularly attracts Guest Conductors and Artists keen to play with the Orchestra. Guest Artists can be located both domestically and internationally creating a potentially global audience for the website.

Barrier Reef Orchestra has identified the current member base to be 13-73. However, Barrier Reef Orchestra want to attract a younger target audience as follows:

Target Audience, 13-40-year old’s.

  • Young families and their high school age children.
  • Music lovers, concert lovers.
  • Educated, sophisticated, cultured.
  • Mid-high disposable incomes.

How

We make it Spunky! The Great Barrier Reef will feature as a key design element and palette reference. Having the reef on our doorstep is a truly stunning attraction for domestic and international visitors alike, so let’s remind them of the Orchestras namesake. Furthermore, the Barrier Reef Orchestra is a prestigious ensemble, our design aims to maintain a prestigious feel and look that the educated, sophisticated, and cultured target audience expects.

And most importantly, our target audience is likely to visit the website on both traditional PC and mobile devices, creating a responsive design will ensure the best customer experience on any device.
