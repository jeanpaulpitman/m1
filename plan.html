Goals

The primary goal of the new website is a 50% increase in revenue from the key income streams; Membership Sales, and Orchestra attendence.

In addition to the primary goal, two core requirements have also been identified:

Core Requirements

  • Attract a younger clientele base.

Success Evaluation

Success evaluation will be measured as a direct increase in online membership sales, and Orchestra attendance numbers. Currently, Orchestra Ticket sales are outsourced to Ticket-Teck with Barrier Reef Orchestra receiving a share of the Ticket Sales for each performance. Whereas, Membership sales is handeled inhouse from the current website.

Both Membership sales, and Orchestra Attendence revenue streams allow quantifiable measurments to track revenue increase. It is expected the Goal of a 50% revenue increase be reached within tweleve months for the website to be deemed successful.

Target Audience

Barrier Reef Orchestra has identified the current member base to be 13-73. However, Barrier Reef Orchestra want to attract a younger audience, 13-40 year olds, young families and their high school age children, University Students, Defence Personel, Doctors, Lawyers.
